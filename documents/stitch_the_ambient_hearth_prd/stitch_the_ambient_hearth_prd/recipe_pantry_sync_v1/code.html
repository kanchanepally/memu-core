Instructions

Step by Step
01

Preparation

Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Toss the heirloom carrots in a heavy-duty roasting pan with olive oil, sea salt, and black pepper.

02

Honey Glaze

In a small bowl, whisk together the wildflower honey and a splash of warm water. Drizzle over the carrots halfway through the roasting process.

03

The Finish

Roast until tender and caramelized (approx. 25-30 mins). Garnish with fresh thyme and a dollop of cold labneh.
