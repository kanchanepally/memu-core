# The Ambient Hearth

## Product Overview

**The Pitch:** A "Mission Control" for family life that lives on your local network, balancing logistical precision with nostalgic warmth. It transforms your tablet or phone into an always-on hearth, surfacing critical daily intel alongside forgotten family memories without ever touching the cloud.

**For:** Privacy-conscious families managing dense schedules who want technology to feel like furniture—solid, reliable, and private.

**Device:** Mobile (Optimized for wall-mounted or counter-top docking).

**Design Direction:** **Soft Industrial**. A tactile, matte dark mode aesthetic. Think high-end audio equipment meets a warm fireplace. Matte charcoal surfaces, brushed brass accents, and monospaced data visualization softened by cream typography.

**Inspired by:** Teenage Engineering (tactile controls), Leica Camera interfaces (purposeful minimalism), and Braun (industrial warmth).

---

## Screens

- **Screen 1: The Hearth (Home):** The default idle state combining the 'Context Carousel' and 'Unified Stream'.
- **Screen 2: Memory Detail:** Immersive photo view with metadata and chat context extracted from local backups.
- **Screen 3: Logistics Command:** Dense, functional calendar view with AI conflict detection and resource management.
- **Screen 4: Node Status:** Hardware-level visualization of the local appliance's health, storage, and ingestion tasks.

---

## Key Flows

**Flow: Morning Briefing**

1. User wakes device -> sees **The Hearth (Home)** in "Morning State".
2. Carousel displays "Traffic to School: Heavy" and "First Event: 8:00 AM".
3. User taps "Traffic" tile -> expands to **Logistics Command** with route options.

**Flow: Evening Reminiscing**

1. Device is docked -> **The Hearth (Home)** auto-switches to "Evening State".
2. Carousel rotates through "On this day" photos.
3. User taps a photo of a birthday -> opens **Memory Detail**.
4. Screen shows photo + local chat log: "Mom: Look at that cake!" (2019).

<summary>Design System</summary>

## Color Palette

- **Primary:** `#D4AF37` - "Brushed Brass" - Active states, primary actions, "On" lights.
- **Background:** `#1A1C20` - "Deep Slate" - The infinite void behind the interface.
- **Surface:** `#25282E` - "Matte Charcoal" - Cards, headers, the carousel background.
- **Text:** `#EAE6D8` - "Warm Cream" - Primary readability, evocative of old paper.
- **Muted:** `#737985` - "Battleship Grey" - Timestamps, secondary metadata, borders.
- **Accent:** `#3B8D65` - "Signal Green" - The 'System Pulse', success states, secure connection.
- **Alert:** `#CD5D4F` - "Oxide Red" - Conflicts, urgent alerts.

## Typography

- **Headings:** **Space Grotesk**, 600, 24px-32px. (Geometric, slightly quirky, technical but friendly).
- **Body:** **Newsreader**, 400, 18px. (Serif, high legibility, brings the "Hearth" warmth).
- **Data/UI:** **JetBrains Mono**, 500, 12px. (Strictly for timestamps, stats, and coordinates).
- **Buttons:** **Space Grotesk**, 500, 14px (Uppercase, tracked out).

**Style notes:**
- **Neumorphism (Subtle):** Very soft inner shadows on "depressed" buttons to mimic physical toggles.
- **Noise:** A 2% monochromatic noise overlay on all backgrounds to prevent banding and add "grain."
- **Borders:** 1px solid borders (`#3A3F47`) on all cards. No drop shadows; everything is flat and flush.

<summary>Screen Specifications</summary>

### Screen 1: The Hearth (Home)

**Purpose:** The central dashboard. Ambiently informs the family of logistical needs (AM) and emotional connections (PM).

**Layout:**
- **Top Bar (48px):** Weather (Left), Time (Center), **System Pulse** (Right).
- **Context Carousel (35% height):** Dominant visual element.
- **Unified Stream (Remaining height):** Vertical scroll below.

**Key Elements:**
- **System Pulse:** A 12px circle, `#3B8D65`. Animates with a slow "breathing" opacity (0.6 to 1.0) every 4s. Label next to it in Mono font: `LOCAL_NODE: ACTIVE`.
- **Context Carousel:** Horizontal swipe container.
    - *Tile 1 (Main):* Large typography. "School Run in 15m". Background is matte charcoal.
    - *Tile 2 (Visual):* Photo memory. Rounded corners (4px).
- **Unified Stream:** A timeline connector line runs down the left.
    - *Item:* "Dad's Birthday" (Calendar icon) paired with "Dad blowing candles 2019" (Photo thumbnail).
    - *Text:* Serif font, `#EAE6D8`, 18px.

**States:**
- **Morning Mode:** Carousel defaults to Weather + Next Event.
- **Evening Mode:** Carousel defaults to Memories + Tomorrow's First Event.

**Interactions:**
- **Swipe Carousel:** Snaps to nearest tile. Haptic feedback on snap (heavy click).
- **Tap Pulse:** Opens **Node Status**.

---

### Screen 2: Memory Detail

**Purpose:** A focused, distraction-free view of a specific memory, enriched with local data context.

**Layout:** Full-screen modal presentation.

**Key Elements:**
- **The Asset:** High-res image centered. Letterboxed if necessary to maintain aspect ratio against `#1A1C20` background.
- **Metadata Badge:** Top left overlay. Mono font. `IMG_2021.JPG • IPHONE 12 • 42MB`.
- **Context Drawer:** Bottom sheet (peeking 120px).
    - **Header:** "Contextual Recall".
    - **Content:** A snippet of a chat log from the same day the photo was taken.
    - *Example:* "Mom: 'Just look at that face!' (14:02 PM)".

**Interactions:**
- **Drag Drawer Up:** Reveals full chat log from that day.
- **Double Tap Image:** Zooms to 200%.
- **Long Press:** "Send to Frame" (Broadcasts to other connected screens).

---

### Screen 3: Logistics Command

**Purpose:** Hard logistics. The "Chief of Staff" view for resolving conflicts and planning.

**Layout:** Grid-based calendar view with a "Conflict Console" at the top.

**Key Elements:**
- **Conflict Console:** A distinct container with a `#CD5D4F` (Red) left border.
    - Text: "Conflict Detected: Soccer Practice overlaps with Piano Lesson."
    - Action Buttons: `[DELEGATE TO MOM]` `[RESCHEDULE PIANO]`.
- **Time Grid:** Vertical day view.
    - **Hours:** Left column, Mono font.
    - **Events:** Blocks in `#25282E` with `#D4AF37` left accent bar.
    - **Ghost Events:** Translucent blocks showing AI suggestions (e.g., "Drive Time: 20m").

**Components:**
- **Event Block:** Height determined by duration. Title in Sans-Serif (Space Grotesk), Location in Serif (Newsreader).

**Responsive:**
- **Mobile:** Single day view.
- **Tablet:** 3-day view.

---

### Screen 4: Node Status

**Purpose:** Reassurance. Proves the hardware is working locally and respecting privacy.

**Layout:** Dashboard of "gauges" and raw data streams.

**Key Elements:**
- **Header:** "Local Node Diagnostic".
- **Storage Ring:** A circular SVG chart using `#D4AF37` stroke. Center text: `4.2TB FREE`.
- **Ingestion Log:** A terminal-like list at the bottom.
    - `[10:02:44] INGESTING: iCloud Backup...`
    - `[10:02:45] PARSING: 42 photos processed locally.`
- **Privacy Shield:** Large icon center screen. Shield symbol. Text: "Air-Gapped Mode: OFF" (or ON).

**Interactions:**
- **Tap 'Purge Cache':** Button triggers a "wiping" animation on the terminal log.

<summary>Build Guide</summary>

**Stack:** HTML + Tailwind CSS v3

**Build Order:**
1. **Screen 1 (The Hearth):** Establishes the Layout Shell, Typography hierarchy, and the crucial "Dark Mode" palette tokens.
2. **Screen 4 (Node Status):** Defines the "Industrial" component set (gauges, mono text, terminal logs) which informs the detail views.
3. **Screen 3 (Logistics):** Implements the complex grid layouts.
4. **Screen 2 (Memory):** Simple layout, focuses on overlay interactions.
